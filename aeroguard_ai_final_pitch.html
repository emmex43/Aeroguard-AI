The Crisis in the Niger-Delta Region

Invisible Threats

Nigeria loses billions annually to pipeline vandalism and crude oil theft. Current monitoring relies on foot patrols or delayed satellite passes, meaning spills often go unnoticed for days.

The remote terrain makes traditional ground security crews slow and ineffective against coordinated criminal syndicates.

The Latency Problem

Chemical Kinematics

Once crude oil is discharged, mass transfer forces cause rapid expansion. Without immediate detection, the oil emulsifies in local waterways, making remediation exponentially more expensive.

Detection Window

AeroGuard shrinks the response time from days to milliseconds. Early identification is the only way to prevent minor leaks from becoming regional ecological disasters.

Introducing AeroGuard

Edge AI Command Center

AeroGuard AI is an autonomous remote monitoring infrastructure that leverages Computer Vision to protect pipeline corridors.

We combine drone telemetry with a fine-tuned YOLOv8 object detection engine to provide instantaneous, localized intelligence on pipeline breaches and slicks.

Protecting Yield

By eradicating detection latency, AeroGuard AI preserves massive amounts of oil volume previously lost to theft and discharge.

Catching spills at the surface sheen stage before emulsification, costs up to 10x less to remediate than post-emulsification cleanup. AeroGuard makes early detection the default, not the exception.
